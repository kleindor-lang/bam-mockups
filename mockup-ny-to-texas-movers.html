Route Intelligence

What Makes Moving from NYC to Texas Different?

Longer Transit Than Southeast Routes

Texas is 1,600–1,900 miles from NYC — longer than Florida, Georgia, or New Jersey. With exclusive service, plan for 4–6 business days. With consolidated, plan for 10–21 days. This is why transit time is one of the most critical factors on this corridor.

Heat & Humidity Protection During Transit

Texas summers regularly exceed 100°F in Dallas and Houston. Electronics, hardwood furniture, wine, plants, and candles can all be damaged in an uncontrolled truck environment. We use appropriate packing and climate-aware routing.

Texas Metro Areas Require Advance Delivery Planning

Dallas has gated communities with strict truck access rules. Houston has no zoning — access varies widely by neighborhood. Austin has dense urban parking restrictions. San Antonio has military base access requirements. We plan all of this before the truck leaves NYC.

Exclusive Transport Gives Better Delivery Control

On a ~1,800-mile corridor, consolidated shipments can experience multiple delays. An exclusive truck goes direct from your NYC pickup to your Texas delivery — no warehousing, no extra handling, no delays from other customers' schedules.

NYC to Texas Moving — FAQ

How much does it cost to move from NYC to Texas?
Studio and 1BR moves to Texas typically start around $2,000; 2–3BR moves run $3,400–$6,000; larger homes start at $5,500+. Cost varies by destination city — Dallas is typically the least expensive, Houston and Austin slightly more. Get a free binding estimate for your exact price.
How long does a move from New York to Texas take?
With exclusive (dedicated truck) service: 4–6 business days to major Texas cities — Dallas is typically 4–5 days, Houston and Austin 5–6 days. With consolidated service, plan for 10–21 days. We confirm your delivery window before the truck departs NYC.
Do you offer guaranteed delivery dates for Texas moves?
Yes — with exclusive transport, we provide a guaranteed delivery window confirmed in writing before departure. With consolidated service, we provide an estimated delivery window rather than a specific date.
Can you store my belongings if my Texas home isn't ready?
Yes. We offer storage-in-transit at our NYC facility and deliver to Texas when your new home is ready. No need to scramble for short-term Texas storage on arrival.
Do you move from all NYC boroughs to Texas?
Yes — we pick up from Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx, Staten Island, and throughout the tri-state area including New Jersey and Connecticut. We coordinate building access and parking permits for all NYC boroughs.
What is the difference between consolidated and exclusive for a NYC to Texas move?
Consolidated: your belongings share truck space with other customers — lower cost but a 10–21 day delivery window. Exclusive: your own dedicated truck, direct service, 4–6 day delivery. For a 1,600–1,900 mile move, we recommend exclusive for anything larger than a 1BR or with a fixed move-in date.
Do you handle packing for NYC to Texas moves?
Yes — full packing, partial packing, and specialty packing (fine art, electronics, wine) are all available. For a long-distance move through Texas heat, professional packing is strongly recommended for fragile or high-value items.
Do you also move from Texas to New York?
Yes. Big Apple Moving handles moves in both directions.

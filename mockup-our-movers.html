Who We Are

A Family-Run NYC Moving Company — 45 Years in the Making

Big Apple Moving has been part of New York City since 1979. What started as a small operation has grown into one of NYC's most trusted moving companies — but the fundamentals haven't changed: we own our trucks and work with designated moving teams directly, so you know exactly who is handling your move.

We are not a broker. We don't hand your job off to an unknown crew at the last minute. When you book with Big Apple Moving, the same team that shows up at your door is the team we stand behind.

Over 45 years, we've completed tens of thousands of NYC moves — from fifth-floor walkups in the West Village to white-glove moves in Tribeca, and long-distance runs to every corner of the country.

Our Hiring Process

How We Hire — Who Makes the BAM Team

Not everyone makes it onto a Big Apple Moving crew. We take hiring seriously because we're sending people into your home.

1

Background Check

Every mover on our team undergoes a thorough background check before their first job. No exceptions — this applies to every crew member, regardless of experience level.

2

Reference Verification

We verify work history and professional references before hiring. We look for people with a track record of reliability, care, and professionalism.

3

Experience Requirements

Our movers bring hands-on moving experience before joining BAM. NYC moving — with its elevators, tight stairwells, and parking constraints — requires specific know-how.

4

Ongoing Performance Review

Our movers are rated on every job based on customer feedback, care with belongings, and professionalism. We keep our standards high by holding the whole team to them.

Training

How We Train — Built for NYC

NYC moving isn't like moving anywhere else. Our team is trained specifically for the challenges of New York: tight hallways, doormen and building managers, Certificate of Insurance requirements, elevator reservations, and the general intensity of city logistics.

✓

Furniture Wrapping & Protection

Every piece of furniture is wrapped before it moves — floors included. Our training covers blanket wrapping, mattress bags, and custom packing for fragile items.

✓

Building Navigation & COI Coordination

We know how NYC buildings work — elevator reservations, loading dock access, building manager sign-offs, and COI submission. Handled before the truck arrives.

✓

Long-Distance Packing Standards

For interstate moves, our training covers additional protection layers — custom crating, climate-sensitive packing, and securing loads for cross-country transit.

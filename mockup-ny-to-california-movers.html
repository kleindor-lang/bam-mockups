NYC to California Moving — FAQ

How much does it cost to move from NYC to California?
Studio and 1BR moves start around $3,000; 2–3BR moves run $5,500–$9,000; larger homes start at $8,000+. These are market-rate ranges — your binding price comes from a survey of your actual inventory, floor access, service level, and CA destination. Get a free quote to see your specific number.
How long does a NYC to California move take?
With exclusive (dedicated truck) service: 7–10 business days door to door — guaranteed delivery window. With consolidated (shared truck) service: 14–21 business days — estimated range. We confirm your delivery window before the truck leaves NYC.
Do you offer guaranteed delivery dates for the NYC-to-California route?
Yes — with exclusive transport, we provide a guaranteed delivery window. This is especially important for a cross-country move where coordinating your California arrival with a lease start date, building move-in appointment, or HOA window matters significantly.
Should I choose consolidated or exclusive for a NYC to California move?
For most 2BR+ moves on this route, we recommend exclusive transport. The price difference is justified by a guaranteed delivery window, faster transit, and no risk of your belongings being delayed by another customer's schedule. For studios and 1BR moves on a flexible timeline, consolidated is a solid, budget-conscious option.
Can you handle packing for a NYC to California move?
Yes — full packing, partial packing, and specialty packing (fine art, wine, antiques, electronics) are all available. For a cross-country move of 2,800+ miles, professional packing significantly reduces the risk of damage through multiple climate zones and road conditions.
Can you store my belongings if I'm not ready in California?
Yes. We offer storage-in-transit at our NYC facility. We hold your shipment and deliver to California when your new home is ready — no need to scramble for California storage on arrival.
Do you pick up from all five NYC boroughs?
Yes — Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx, Staten Island, and throughout the tri-state area including New Jersey and Connecticut.
Do you also move from California back to New York?
Yes. Big Apple Moving handles moves in both directions, including California to NYC relocations. We provide licensed interstate moving, binding estimates, storage options, and coordinated delivery into all five NYC boroughs — the same level of service in reverse.

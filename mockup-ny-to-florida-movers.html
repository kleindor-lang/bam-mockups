Route Intelligence

What Makes Moving from NYC to Florida Different?

Hurricane Season Planning

Florida moves between June and November can be affected by tropical storms. We adjust delivery windows accordingly and may hold shipments at our NYC facility during active storm warnings.

High-Rise COI Requirements in South Florida

Buildings in Miami, Brickell, Fort Lauderdale, and Boca Raton almost universally require COIs and advance elevator reservations. We coordinate all building requirements before the truck leaves NYC.

Snowbird Season Demand (Oct–Apr)

October through April is peak season for NYC to Florida moves. Exclusive truck availability and consolidated freight space both tighten during this period. Request your estimate 4–6 weeks out.

Heat & Humidity Protection

Florida heat and humidity can damage hardwood furniture, electronics, artwork, wine, and plants. We use climate-appropriate packing materials and protect high-value items with extra padding.

FAQ

NYC to Florida Moving — FAQ

How much does it cost to move from NYC to Florida?
Studio and 1BR moves to Florida start around $2,000 for Northern FL; South Florida runs $3,500–$5,500 for the same size. 2–3BR moves range from $3,500–$8,500. Larger homes: $5,000–$8,000+ for North FL, $8,000–$13,000 for South FL.
How long does a move from New York to Florida take?
Exclusive: 2–3 business days to Jacksonville and Orlando/Tampa; 3–4 days to Miami/Fort Lauderdale/Boca Raton. Consolidated: 7–21 days. All windows confirmed in writing before departure.
Do you offer guaranteed delivery dates for Florida moves?
Yes — exclusive transport provides a guaranteed delivery window confirmed in writing before departure. Consolidated provides an estimated window. Hurricane season (June–Nov) and snowbird season (Oct–Apr) windows include appropriate buffers.
Can you store my belongings if my Florida home isn't ready?
Yes. Storage-in-transit at our NYC facility, delivered to Florida on your confirmed schedule. Common during snowbird season when closings shift.
Do you move from all NYC boroughs to Florida?
Yes — Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx, Staten Island, and the tri-state area (NJ, CT). We coordinate building access, parking permits, and COI paperwork for all boroughs.
What are the biggest challenges with a NYC to Florida move?
(1) South Florida COI and elevator requirements; (2) snowbird season availability (Oct–Apr peak); (3) hurricane season weather delays (June–Nov); (4) heat protection for electronics, wine, and hardwood furniture.
What is the difference between consolidated and exclusive for a NYC to Florida move?
Consolidated: shared truck space, lower cost, 7–21 day window. Exclusive: dedicated truck, 2–4 day direct delivery. For anything larger than a 1BR or with a fixed move-in date, exclusive is recommended.
Do you handle packing for NYC to Florida moves?
Yes — full, partial, and specialty packing (fine art, electronics, wine, antiques). For the Florida climate, professional packing is strongly recommended for fragile or high-value items.
Do you also move from Florida to New York?
Yes. Big Apple Moving handles Florida to New York relocations — same licensed carrier service, binding estimates, storage options, and delivery coordination. Pickup from all Florida regions, delivery into all five NYC boroughs and tri-state area.
